Long Form

Long format editing is about depth and storytelling, perfect for documentaries and interviews.

It allows creators to explore intricate narratives, build emotional connections, and provide a comprehensive look at the subject matter.

Short Form

Short form editing is fast-paced and ideal for social media, ads, and quick storytelling.

It focuses on grabbing attention quickly, delivering a clear message, and leaving a strong impression within a limited timeframe.

This style often relies on dynamic visuals, snappy transitions, and engaging audio to captivate audiences and drive action in seconds.
